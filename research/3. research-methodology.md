# Research Methodology

## Objective

To identify repeatable YouTube content strategies that can support growth for B2B SaaS companies.

## Expert Selection Criteria

Experts were selected based on:

* Demonstrated audience growth
* Consistent content production
* Practical implementation experience
* Strong reputation within marketing or creator ecosystems

## Data Sources

* LinkedIn posts
* YouTube videos
* Public interviews
* Creator-led educational content

## Collection Process

1. Identify experts
2. Collect recent content
3. Categorize by topic
4. Extract recurring frameworks
5. Compare across experts
6. Synthesize findings into a draft playbook

## Scope

* 10 experts
* 30 LinkedIn posts
* 20 YouTube videos
